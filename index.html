Welcome to my personal sight!

I'm a 23 year old Ohioan who wants to showcase my programming work here.

I believe in making software free and libre, allowing anyone to use and modify it to their own liking with little to no charge.
Privacy, freedom of expression, and building a freer/more open internet are fundamentals for me.
As of right now, I am a hobbyist that's looking to learn more and share whatever I like/root for.
 
Hate and bigotry are not welcome here either (it stifles others' free expression and is therefore anti-freedom).
